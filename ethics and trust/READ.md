## Experiment: AI UX Ethics Analyzer

**Date started:** 2026-04-19
**Status:** active

**What I'm trying to learn**
Whether Claude can analyze visual and prototype artifacts (user flows, 
Figma screens, PDFs, HTML prototypes) and assess them against AI UX 
ethics parameters — specifically Trust, Explainability, Transparency, 
Fairness, and Accountability — in a way that is actionable for UX 
designers rather than researchers or engineers.

The secondary question is whether the output can meaningfully 
distinguish between what a UX designer can directly control, what 
requires cross-functional collaboration, and what is outside design's 
scope entirely.

**What I built**
Dashboard exploration concepts in Claude Code Design

**Result**
Wireframes with options
